## THREAD PROJECT: Psychological Investigation Portfolio

### Overview
Progressive semester-long project where students conduct a psychological investigation, building research literacy and scientific thinking skills across 4 checkpoints.

### Checkpoints

**Checkpoint 1 (Module 1 - Week 2):** Research Question & Design (50 pts)
- Choose psychological phenomenon to investigate
- Formulate research question
- Identify appropriate research method
- Create basic research design
- **PTS:** Scientific Reasoning (PTS1), Research Literacy (CLO2)

**Checkpoint 2 (Module 3 - Week 6):** Literature Review & Hypothesis (50 pts)
- Review 3-5 empirical studies
- Synthesize existing research
- Develop specific hypothesis
- Justify methodology choice
- **PTS:** Scientific Reasoning (PTS1), Critical Evaluation (PTS6)

**Checkpoint 3 (Module 5 - Week 10):** Data Analysis & Results (50 pts)
- Collect/analyze data (simulated or real)
- Create data visualizations
- Interpret findings
- Discuss limitations
- **PTS:** Data Literacy (PTS5), Scientific Reasoning (PTS1)

**Checkpoint 4 (Module 7 - Week 14):** Final Report & Presentation (50 pts)
- Complete APA-style research report
- Include biopsychosocial integration
- Discuss implications
- Present findings (written or video)
- **PTS:** ALL six PTS skills demonstrated
